Want to Volunteer?

Anyone can volunteer at PV's Robotics Club! You don't need any engineering, programming, or business experience, and you don't need to be a PV Parent in order to volunteer!

Any technical or handy person willing to learn something new along side the students can be a mentor! Students need guidance in project planning, software development, and budgeting. Mentors help is invaluable, and PV Robotics is always thankful for it.

If you're not comfortable being a mentor but still want to help, volunteers can help with T-shirts, meals, travel, shipping logistics, and an especially to organize other volunteers. We even need shopping experience because we need to buy a lot of materials and tools on a budget. Just being on-hand at meetings to help with whatever comes up is welcome!

In addition to this, you also need to either already have your proper clearances or receive your clearances by contacting the Human Resources Office. Contact Susan Angel (sangel@pvsd.org) or Karen Zucker (kzucker@pvsd.org) for more information.

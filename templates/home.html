About us

The Perkiomen Valley Retrobotics team is a club meant to combine multiple different skill sets to build robots. The club allows good teamwork, perseverance and hard work.Teams work together against a time a time constraints in order to build a robot to complete tasks at the FIRST Robotics Competition. An international high school robotics competition that gives students real world engineering experience.
